# CyberTOMP Code of Conduct

## 1. Purpose
CyberTOMP is an open framework for cybersecurity management based on formal research. We aim to maintain an inclusive, collaborative, and respectful community.

## 2. Principles
- **Respect**: Treat everyone with courtesy, regardless of experience, identity, or background.
- **Constructive Collaboration**: Criticism should be technical and aimed at improving the project.
- **Transparency**: Communicate intentions and contributions clearly and honestly.

## 3. Acceptable Behavior
- Share knowledge and help others.
- Report issues and propose improvements with technical arguments.
- Acknowledge the work of others.

## 4. Unacceptable Behavior
- Offensive, discriminatory, or aggressive language.
- Spam, excessive self-promotion, or irrelevant content.
- Personal attacks or sabotage of the project.

## 5. Consequences
Violations of this code may result in:
- Warnings.
- Temporary or permanent contribution bans.
- Removal from community channels.

## 6. Reporting
If you observe inappropriate behavior:
- Contact the administrators at [email/contact].
- Provide details of the incident (date, context, links).

## 7. Attribution
This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1
